About Us

The I Solution International Pvt. Ltd. is an independently privately owned and both locally and internationally operated insurance agency situated in Kathmandu, Nepal. Started in 2018, as Nepal’s first and one and only insurance agency as a company, I Solution aims to help people find affordable and reliable insurance products from a variety of trusted carriers.

As an “Independent” insurance agency, we’re not tied to any one insurance carrier. In other words, we can research rates and coverage options from several top insurance companies to find the best policies for you and can help to find the perfect combination of coverage and price to fit your unique needs. At I Solution International Pvt. Ltd., we believe our customers should have a voice in designing the coverage they need to protect themselves, their families and businesses. We will work with our customers to help identify insurance needs, offer coverage choices and flexibility in the design of a cost-effective insurance plan to meet those needs.
