The I Solution International Pvt. Ltd. is an independently privately owned and both locally and internationally operated insurance agency situated in Kathmandu, Nepal. Started in 2018, as Nepal’s first and one and only insurance agency as a company, I Solution aims to help people find affordable and reliable insurance products from a variety of trusted carriers.

As an “Independent” insurance agency, we’re not tied to any one insurance carrier. In other words, we can research rates and coverage options from several top insurance companies to find the best policies for you and can help to find the perfect combination of coverage and price to fit your unique needs. At I Solution International Pvt. Ltd., we believe our customers should have a voice in designing the coverage they need to protect themselves, their families and businesses. We will work with our customers to help identify insurance needs, offer coverage choices and flexibility in the design of a cost-effective insurance plan to meet those needs.

I Solution International Pvt. Ltd. was started by professionals who have been serving in the insurance industry for more than 10 years. Anjan Bhandari, being the Chairman of the company who is a well-reputed and nationally and internationally recognized personality in the insurance industry partnered with Karna Bahadur Shahi and Suresh Pokhrel to start the Nepal’s first insurance agency firm. The three partners Anjan Bhandari as Chairman, Karna Bahadur Shahi as Managing Director and Suresh Pokhrel as Marketing Manager established I Solution Pvt. Ltd. to revolutionize and bring new trend of insurance agency as firm in the context of Nepal and to serve the people by providing them competitive and comprehensive Personal and Commercial lines of insurance coverage. Although all three partners were successful in their own right, they realized they could better reach their goals and objectives through combining resources and working together as a team. It is this teamwork concept along with a sincere desire to serve that has set the foundation for I Solution International Pvt. Ltd.

We continue to look ahead and plan for future development. This feeling of confidence relates to I Solution’ Corporate Philosophy: emphasize the need to plan ahead and take advantage of trends and changes occurring in the Insurance Industry. I Solution does this for our customers, as well as our carriers

As an independent insurance agency, the companies we represent offer financial security, excellent products and claims service. So, we invite you to contact us to review coverage options for auto, home, life, health, business, and more.

We provide free, no hassle and no obligation insurance quotes to help you make an informed decision.

We do the shopping and comparing to save your time and money!
